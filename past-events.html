Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, ICNFA, was held on August 19 - 21, 2018, in Madrid, Spain. ICNFA is one of the 4 conferences that make up the World Congress on New Technologies (NewTech'18). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Dr. Monika Bojko, for the paper entitled: "Acclimatization of Diatom Phaeodactylum Tricornutum to Long-Term Environmental Temperature and Light Intensity Changes" ;

  • Dr. Hong-Chan Chang, for the paper entitled: "Energy Consumption Level Prediction Based on Classification Approach with Machine Learning Technique" ;

  • Atasi Dan, for the paper entitled: "Evaluation of Spectral Selectivity of High Temperature Multilayer Solar Absorber Thin Film" ;

  • Siddharth Dash, for the paper entitled: "A Mechanism Using Shape-Memory Alloys to Assist Partially Paralysed Patients in Walking With a Natural Gait" ;

  • Dr. Praveen Kumar, for the paper entitled: "Surface Modified InGaN/Si(111) Photoanode For Efficient Photoelectrochemical Water-Splitting" ;

  • Manhattan Lebrun, for the paper entitled: "Biochar Amendment Associated to Compost and/or Iron in Order to Improve Lead and Arsenic Soil Stabilization and Salix Viminalis Growth" ;

  • Keyong Nam Lee, for the paper entitled: "Tri-package White LEDs as a High-Color-Quality Lighting Source with Cd-free Quantum Dots" ;

  • Dorothea Summer, for the paper entitled: "Inhibition and Stimulation of Different Dechlorinating Bacterial Cultures Using Iron Particles" ;
