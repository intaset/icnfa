Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, ICNFA, was held on June 6 - 8, 2017, in Rome, Italy. ICNFA is one of the 4 conferences that make up the World Congress on New Technologies (NewTech). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Dr. Barbara Paci
    for the paper entitled: "Stability Enhancement in OPV: In-Situ Studies of Plasmonic Devices"
  • Dr. Xingding Zhou
    for the paper entitled: "Strain Development for Water Assimilable Organic Carbon Detection"
  • Dr. Chengci Chen
    for the paper entitled: "Conservation Tillage and Nitrogen Management for Sugarbeet Production"
  • Ildikó Fekete-Kertész
    for the paper entitled: "The Combined Ecotoxicity of nTiO2 and Coexisting Micropollutants in the Aquatic Ecosystem"
  • Dr. Denisa Ficai
    for the paper entitled: "Collagen/Hydroxyapatite Composite Supports for Bone Tissue Engineering"
  • Mr. Vinod Kadam
    for the paper entitled: "Bilayer Electrospun Nanofibre Structures to Improve Quality Factor in Air Filtration"
